— PARCHMENT MAP ITERATIONS —

Gold leaf accents, ornate borders, decorated initial letters. Richer colors on parchment. A royal cartographer's masterwork.

D · DARK VELLUM

Dark aged leather background with gold and silver ink. Mysterious and atmospheric. Buildings glow softly on hover.
